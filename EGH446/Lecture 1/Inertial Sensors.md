### Description
Sensors that measure motion of the body they are attached to, without external references.

- **Gyroscopes**: Measure angular velocity (rate of rotation).

	- Exists in three common configurations: Mechanical, optical and MEMS. 
### Characteristics
- Self-contained and environment-independent
- Do not rely on external signals
- Classified as passive sensors

### Applications
- Aircraft, spacecraft, drones
- Robotics
- Navigation for mobile devices and vehicles
