#Sensors #GPS #Navigation

This week covers the **Global Positioning System (GPS)**, a space-based navigation system that provides absolute positioning and time information in all weather.

While the US-maintained NAVSTAR system is the most common, other global constellations include Russia's GLONASS, Europe's Galileo, and China's Compass/BeiDou.
