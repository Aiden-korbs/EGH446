## Scope
- Introduces basic Kalman filtering concepts.
- Companion notes available.
- Main references:
  - Y. Bar-Shalom, X. Rong Li, T. Kirubarajan — *Estimation with Applications to Tracking and Navigation*.
  - R.G. Brown, P.Y.C. Hwang — *Introduction to Random Signal and Applied Kalman Filtering*.
  - Mathworks — "Understand The Kalman Filter".
  - B.D.O. Anderson, J. Moore — *Optimal Filtering* (freely available online).

## Why study Kalman Filters?
- Widely used in guidance, navigation, and control.
- Examples:
  - Apollo missions (mid-course guidance).
  - Advanced aircraft and robotics.
  - Mars rovers.
